## Investment Objective

Neev Navigate delivers diversified multi-asset growth using only index funds. It uses the same allocation system, deployment logic, and momentum rebalancing as Accelerate — but eliminates fund-picking risk entirely. Navigate proves that most of Neev's outperformance comes from the system (allocation + momentum), not from stock picking.

---

## Investment Strategy

1. **Reserve base**: All surplus first flows into Neev Reserve (20% liquid + 80% arbitrage)
2. **Deployment trigger**: Once Reserve exceeds 3x monthly surplus, 25% of excess deploys monthly into the diversified index portfolio
3. **Core allocation (40%)**: Nifty 50 index — stable large-cap foundation
4. **Satellite allocation (60%)**: Spread across 4 index funds (15% each):
   - Nifty Next 50 — large-cap breadth
   - Nifty Midcap 150 — mid-cap market coverage
   - Nifty Midcap 150 — replaces active flexi-cap with broader mid-cap index
   - S&P 500 — US equity diversification
5. **Quarterly momentum rebalancing**: Same system as Accelerate — satellite weights tilted toward funds with stronger recent momentum
6. **Zero fund-picking risk**: No active manager dependency. Returns come purely from market beta + allocation + momentum

## Key Risks

1. **Full market exposure**: Index funds capture all of the downside during corrections — no active manager to defensively rotate
2. **Mid-cap index concentration**: 30% effective allocation to Nifty Midcap 150 (2 satellite slots) adds volatility during mid-cap corrections
3. **No alpha potential**: By definition, Navigate cannot outperform the market — returns equal market beta adjusted for allocation and momentum
4. **Tracking error**: Index funds may slightly underperform their benchmarks due to tracking error, cash drag, and expense ratios
5. **International currency risk**: S&P 500 allocation is subject to USD/INR exchange rate fluctuations
6. **Momentum rebalancing in mean-reverting markets**: If recent momentum reverses sharply, the quarterly tilt may amplify short-term losses
7. **Drawdown similar to Accelerate**: -6.39% max drawdown — investors must be comfortable with this level of volatility
